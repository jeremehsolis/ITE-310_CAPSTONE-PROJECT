The Project

The PHINMA-UPang Room Assignment and Scheduling System aims to solve the discrepancy in the population of students that is currently being experienced by the Registrar of PHINMA-University of Pangasinan. The presence of our system will make it easier to monitor and assign rooms and also avoid discrepancies. PHINMA University of Pangasinan is one of the many institutions that utilizes Information Technology (IT) in making lives better, not just for the students, but also for their offices.

The System

The system is equipped with capabilities that can create a database for archiving documents and data. In case of changes, it enables the end-user to add, edit, delete, save, and update records or information. Reports including class schedules, faculty schedules, room schedules, room lists, and section lists can be generated using it.

The Problem

Universities spend a lot of time and money every year on the challenging and time-consuming task of creating a course schedule. To address this issue, we created a computer program. The PC-based tool allows the user to select the goals and constraints from a wide range of options. It includes an easy-to-use interface that allows the user to enter the necessary information and get the necessary results. A group of heuristic algorithms form the foundation of the optimization process. The fundamental algorithm is a Tabu Search technique, for which a number of different approaches have been created and put to the test.
